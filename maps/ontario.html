Ontario Services

This is a proof of concept map created for the American Citizen Services section of the U.S. Consulate in Toronto to demonstrate how to geographically visualize and access information regarding key contacts and locations. implemented Leaflet.markercluster so you can mouse over a cluster to see the bounds of its children and click a cluster to zoom to those bounds. This map also incorporates a layer switcher to only display markers from selected categories.
