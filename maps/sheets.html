US Tornados June 2012

A simple map of tornados in the U.S. during June 2012. The point data is stored in a Google Sheets workbook and is accessed using jQuery and the Spreadsheets Data API. I used leaflet.js to add markers over CartoDB Positron map tiles. The use of a minimalist custom designed tornado marker creates a unique heat map like effect.
